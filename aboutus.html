About

Some might say that the idea of fishy confections is abominable-- we're here to tell you: we make them. Every day we come to work hoping to do two things: make cookies and get the smell of fish off our hands. Salmon cookies are a family recipe passed down from Pat's grandfather, irascible cockswain Cornelious Montegomery Baker, who devised the concept whilst sailing the ocean blue in search of the great sockeye salmon who stole his lucky hat. He dedicated his life to salmon, knowing he would see his rival once more because it would be wearing his hat. This is the same erratic behaviour you'll see in our bakery every day.
